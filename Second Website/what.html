WHAT NOT TO DO

DO NOT UNDER ANY CIRCUMSTANCES:

  1. Set a zombie on fire
  2. Have long hair
  3. Think a location is safe without checking
  4. Make a ton of noise
  5. Search for the reason behind that mysterious noise
  6. Hide in a basement
  7. Keep zombie bodies laying around
  8. Not tell anyone if you get bitten
  9. Think you are out of danger
