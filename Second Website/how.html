HOW TO RELAX

Here you will free your mind, do not worry. On this page you will not think about any negatives and be positive. Believe you will survive and it will happen. Perform some of these tips and you mind will be freed.

  1. Take deep breaths
  2. Listen to music
  3. Meditate
  4. Relax your muscles
  5. Take a SAFE walk because it is the zombie apocalypse after all
  6. Take a nap
  7. Play with a pet and if you don't have one play with your pet rock
  8. Talk to a friend
  9. Count
  10. Look at relaxing pictures
  11. Think happy thoughts
  12. Remember all the things you love and what you're living for
  13. Do some calming yoga
